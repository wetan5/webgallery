My Gallery

I am a high school senior who is taking Computer Art this year. I am in a public high school in San Francisco, California. Belows are four pieces of art works I created this year.

Reflections of your work

We learned a lot about art designing in computer. These four pieces of art works were created by me during the year using different applications like Adobe Photoshop and Adobe Illustrator. For the first one, I used photoshop to replace the sky with an image of broken glass and then put an eye in the middle. The second one is the anpanman I made myself. The next one is the rockstar I made in Illustrator by looking at a real rockstar bottle. For the last one, I use Illustrator to combine a lion mouth and an apple to make it look like an apple with big mouth.
